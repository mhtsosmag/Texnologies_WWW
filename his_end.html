His End

Kazantzidis died of a brain tumor on 14 September 2001.[10] His death was an emotional event for Greece, as attested to by the many obituaries in appreciation of his life and work.[11] Kazantzidis was given a state funeral through the streets of Elefsina (26 km from Athens) which was broadcast live on Greek television. His music was also beloved by the Greek diaspora all over the world, capturing their feelings in the difficult post-war period. He was commemorated on a Greek postage stamp in 2010.
